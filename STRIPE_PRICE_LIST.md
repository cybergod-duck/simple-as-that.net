## Tier Feature Breakdown

### Tier 1 – Landing Page ($79)
- **Hero**: High-Conversion Above-the-Fold Hero (H1, Subtitle, 1 Primary CTA)
- **Features**: Inline 3-Point "Why Choose Us" Highlights
- **Trust**: Simple text-based testimonial quote
- **Integrations**: Basic Lead Capture Form (routes directly to client email)
- **Pages**: Single landing page optimized for immediate conversions

### Tier 2 – Professional ($199)
- **Hero**: Split-Screen Hero with Industry-Specific Image
- **Features**: 2-Column Icon Grid (4 value propositions)
- **Trust**: 3-Column Simple Review Slider
- **Contact**: Basic Footer + Dedicated "Contact Us" Page
- **Pages**: Up to 3 Pages (Home, Services, Contact)

### Tier 3 – Business ($349)
- **Hero**: Dynamic Hero with Lead Capture floating form
- **Features**: Zig-Zag Layout (Alternating Text/Image rows)
- **Trust**: Dedicated "Client Success" section with logos + reviews
- **Integrations**: Intermediate Form mapping to local CRM/Email
- **Pages**: Up to 3 Pages (Home, About, Contact)
- **★ Includes**: Simple AI Plus ($29/mo subscription)

### Tier 4 – Premium ($549)
- **Hero**: High-end Video Background Hero
- **Features**: Interactive Hover Cards mapping exact pain points from data.csv
- **Trust**: 3-Column Masonry Review Grid
- **Integrations**: Advanced Lead Capture & Basic calendar integration
- **Pages**: Up to 4 Pages (Home, About, Services, Contact)
- **★ Includes**: Simple AI Plus ($29/mo subscription)

### Tier 5 – Enterprise ($849)
- **Hero**: Premium 3D floating Dashboard Mockup or Custom Video Hero
- **Features**: Complex interactive scroll-animations (Timeline of Services) + Interactive Hover Cards with Micro-animations
- **Trust**: Video Testimonial blocks & Dynamic State-specific Trust Badges
- **Integrations**: Enterprise Booking (Calendly mapping) & direct CRM Webhook
- **Bonus**: Automatically includes Custom Logo & State Compliance Pack
- **Pages**: Up to 6 Pages (Home, About, Services, Trust, Contact) + automated Blog infrastructure setup
- **★ Includes**: Simple AI Plus ($29/mo subscription)
